WHAT IS 'LOST AND DEAD CLASSIC'?

Lost and Dead Classic is the latest project we've been working on. We've taken the classic Lost and Dead (before the 0.4 overhaul), added a few more goodies, bits and bobs, changed some textures around, taken out all the stolen content, and we've made sure it is much better than the original Lost and Dead which was released on ModDB early access in October 2017.

WHAT IS 'LOST AND DEAD - EPISODE IV: AWAKENING'?

Lost and Dead - Episode IV: Awakening is what we will be working on after Lost and Dead Classic is ready. It is part of an episodic trilogy. The episodic trilogy is inspired by what George Lucas did with Star Wars.
